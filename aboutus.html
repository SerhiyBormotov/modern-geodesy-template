ABOUT US

let us introduce ourselves

We are a team of young but experienced surveyor engineers.

We joined together to bring the level of geodetic services to a qualitatively new level.

Our company has existed for only 5 years, but during this time we have been able to prove that those who do their work honestly, qualitatively and efficiently do not have a shortage of orders. During this time, our specialists successfully carried out work on more than 2,000 objects.

4 main principles, that allow us to remain among the market leaders:

  1. HONESTY. We are always honest with our customers. We never take orders that we cannot fulfill in full. We never artificially increase the cost of work. We are not trying to trick and evade.
  2. PEDANTICITY. We always adhere to the agreed terms and conditions, down to the last detail.
  3. HARDWORKING We love our work. Ready to work on weekends and holidays, overtime and without a schedule. We are not afraid of difficult tasks and do not run away from difficulties.
  4. DEVELOPMENT In order to always stay on the cutting edge of new technologies in geodesy, we constantly update our material and technical base whenever possible, and train our specialists.
